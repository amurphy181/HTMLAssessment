Sportsball is the name of the game! People run up and down a field in order to move a ball between two distinct zones, until they swap sides and score in the area that was previously coveted by the other team. And they are paid millions upon millions of dollars to do this! In my next life, I'll chase balls better than any other person on my path to enlightenment.

But why? Some feel that it's a way to wage war against your neighbors without having to cause any immediate lethal damage. That unseemly aspect is handled several decades down the road by brain injuries.

It's all just crazy to me.
